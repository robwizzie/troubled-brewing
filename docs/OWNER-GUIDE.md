# Owner Guide — Editing Your Website

A plain-English, printable reference for Tom, Cat & Katie. This mirrors the in-app Help Center at **`/admin/help`**. You never need to touch code to run your site.

## Logging in

1. Go to **troublebrewingcoffeehouse.com/admin**
2. Enter your email and password (each of you has your own login).
3. You'll land on the **Dashboard**. From here you can edit pages, the menu, hours, events, your team, reviews, and more.

> 💡 You can do all of this from an iPad behind the counter.

## The golden rules

- **Draft vs. Published.** Changes start as a **Draft** — only you can see them. The public site shows the **Published** version. When you're happy, click **Publish**. Nothing goes live until you publish.
- **Preview before publishing.** Use **Preview** to see exactly how a page will look with your changes before they go live.
- **You can undo.** Every change is saved in **History**. If something looks wrong, open History and click **Restore** on an earlier version.
- **It saves as you type.** A safety copy is kept in your browser, so a closed tab won't lose your work.

## Common tasks

### Add or edit a menu item
**Menu Manager** → **+ Add item** (or click an existing one). Fill in name, description, price, category, and any dietary tags (gluten-free, vegan). Upload a photo if you like. Toggle **Available** off to hide something temporarily (e.g. sold out). Click **Save**, then **Publish**.

### Update your hours / add a holiday closure
**Hours Editor** → change the open/close times in the weekly grid. For a holiday, click **+ Add holiday** and pick the date, a label ("Closed for Thanksgiving"), and either new hours or "Closed all day." Save and Publish.

> The homepage welcome sign shows **Open now / Closed** with today's closing (or next opening) time automatically, straight from these hours — always on New Jersey time, wherever the visitor is. There's nothing extra to update.

### Change the "Current Drink Specials" link on the homepage
**Page Editor** → **Home** → the **Gallery Wall hero** section → **Specials link text** / **Specials link URL**. Out of the box it says "Current Drink Specials" and opens your menu on the **Specialty** tab (`/menu#specials`). Point it anywhere — `/menu#seasonal` for the seasonal list, or a full URL (e.g. an Instagram post announcing this week's special).

### Pick a frame style for a homepage frame
**Page Editor** → **Home** → the **Gallery Wall hero** section → each frame has a **Frame style** menu with vintage looks matched to your real wall — "Grand gold (ornate)", "Black + wide white mat", "Thin brass on a chain", "Gold oval (specimen)", and more. Like the shop wall, it looks best when no two frames share a style.

### Add an event
**Events Manager** → **+ Add event** → title, date, time, description, optional photo. Past events drop off automatically.

### Add a Troublemaker (team member)
**Troublemakers** → **+ Add Troublemaker** → photo, name, role, a short bio, and the fun facts (favorite local food spot, movie, book, show, music artist). Drag the order, or mark someone inactive when they move on.

### Add a Gallery Wall story
**Gallery Manager** → **+ Add piece** → photo of the framed art, a title, and the fun story behind it. This is the page that tells the tale of your wall.

### Add a local business you support
**Local Love** → **+ Add business** → name, category, a sentence about them, a link to their site, optional photo. (Tip: ask them to link back to you — it helps both of you show up on Google.)

### Add or feature a review
**Testimonials** → **+ Add testimonial** → paste the quote, the reviewer's first name, and a star rating. Mark your favorites **Featured**. Your live Google star rating and a rotating set of Google reviews appear automatically.

### Change a page's layout
**Page Editor** → pick a page → you'll see its **sections** in order. Edit any section's text/images inline, use the up/down arrows to reorder, the eye icon to hide/show, or **+ Add section** to add a new block. Publish when ready.

### Swap the homepage look
**Quick Blocks** → **Homepage concept** → choose **Gallery Wall**, **Warm Storefront**, or **Cozy Editorial**. Great for trying a seasonal feel.

### Read messages from customers
**Inbox** → contact and catering form submissions land here. Filter by type, mark read/unread.

### The announcement banner
**Quick Blocks** → **Announcement banner** → type a short message (e.g. "Closed early Sat for a private event") and turn it on. It shows at the top of every page until you turn it off.

## Photos: keep them snappy
Upload straight from your phone — the site automatically shrinks large photos so your pages stay fast. Landscape shots usually look best for heroes and cards.

## Need help?
The in-app **Help Center** (`/admin/help`) has step-by-step guides with screenshots and a short walkthrough video. If you're ever stuck, reach out to your developer — and remember, **History → Restore** can undo almost anything.
