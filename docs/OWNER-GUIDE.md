# Owner Guide — Editing Your Website

A plain-English, printable reference for Tom, Cat & Katie. This mirrors the in-app Help Center at **`/admin/help`**. You never need to touch code to run your site.

## Logging in

1. Go to **troublebrewingcoffeehouse.com/admin**
2. Enter your email and password (each of you has your own login).
3. You'll land **right on your website, in editing mode**. That's the whole trick: what you see is your real site, and you edit it by clicking on it.

> 💡 You can do all of this from an iPad behind the counter.

## The one idea that runs everything

**Click the thing you want to change.** Hover anywhere on the page and it gets an outline with a little label ("Hero banner", "Menu", "Text block"). Click it, and a panel opens on the right with its words and photos. As you type, the page updates in front of you.

## The golden rules

- **It saves by itself.** Everything you type is saved automatically as a **private draft** — only you can see it. Close the tab, come back tomorrow, it's still there.
- **Nothing goes live until you press Publish.** The **Publish** button (top right) shows how many changes are waiting. Press it when you're happy.
- **You can undo.** Select a section and open **History & restore** — pick an earlier version and click Restore. (Restoring is itself undoable.)
- **Check the phone view.** The 📱 button in the top bar shows the honest phone layout. 🖥 goes back to desktop.

## Common tasks

### Change words or photos on any page
Pick the page from the dropdown in the top bar (or just click a link on the page — the editor follows you). Click the section, edit in the panel, watch it change. Publish when ready.

### Add or edit a menu item
Go to the **Menu** page, click the menu, press **Manage menu →** in the panel. **+ Add item** (or click one to edit): name, description, price, category, dietary tags, photo. Toggle **Available** off to hide something temporarily (e.g. sold out).

### Update your hours / add a holiday closure
**⚙ Settings → Hours** → change the open/close times in the weekly grid. For a holiday, click **+ Add holiday** and pick the date, a label ("Closed for Thanksgiving"), and either new hours or "Closed all day."

> The homepage welcome sign shows **Open now / Closed** with today's closing (or next opening) time automatically, straight from these hours — always on New Jersey time. Nothing extra to update.

### Add an event
Go to the **Events** page, click the events list, press **Manage events →**. Title, date, time, description, optional photo. Past events drop off automatically.

### Add a Troublemaker (team member)
Go to the **Troublemakers** page, click the team grid, press **Manage Troublemakers →**. Photo, name, role, short bio, fun facts. Mark someone inactive when they move on (keeps their record).

### Add a Gallery Wall story / a local business
Same pattern: go to the page, click the section, press the **Manage —** button in the panel.

### Add or feature a review
Go to the **Reviews** page, click the testimonials wall, press **Manage testimonials →**. Paste the quote, the reviewer's first name, stars, and mark your favorites **Featured**. Your live Google rating appears automatically.

### Rearrange, hide, or remove sections
With nothing selected, the right panel lists every section on the page in order: ▲▼ to reorder, 👁 to hide/show, 🗑 to remove, **+ Add a section** at the bottom. These apply right away; hidden sections stay ghosted in the editor so you can bring them back.

### Swap the homepage look
On the **Home** page, click the big hero at the top. The panel shows **Homepage look** — Gallery Wall, Warm Storefront, Cozy Editorial, or Modern Coffee. Pick one and the page swaps instantly. Each look has its own words and photos you can edit right below.

### Change the "Current Drink Specials" link on the homepage
With the **Gallery Wall** look active, click the hero → **Specials link text** / **Specials link URL**. Out of the box it opens your menu on the **Specialty** tab (`/menu#specials`). Point it anywhere — `/menu#seasonal`, or a full URL (e.g. an Instagram post).

### Pick a frame style for a homepage frame
Click the hero (Gallery Wall look) → each frame has a **Frame style** menu with vintage looks matched to your real wall. Like the shop wall, it looks best when no two frames share a style.

### Read messages from customers
**⚙ Settings → Inbox** — contact and catering submissions land here. Filter by type, mark read/unread. (An unread count shows on the Inbox link.)

### The announcement banner
**⚙ Settings → Quick Blocks → Announcement banner** — type a short message ("Closed early Sat for a private event") and turn it on. It shows at the top of every page until you turn it off.

## Photos: keep them snappy
Upload straight from your phone — the site automatically shrinks large photos so your pages stay fast. Landscape shots usually look best for heroes and cards.

## Need help?
The in-app **Help Center** (⚙ Settings → Help Center) has step-by-step guides and a short walkthrough video. If you're ever stuck, reach out to your developer — and remember, **History → Restore** can undo almost anything.
